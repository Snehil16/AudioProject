Story

The story "Fear in the Skies: A Flight Emergency" tells about a flight that experiences severe turbulence while flying from Abu Dhabi to New York. The soundscape of the story is designed to recreate the ambiance of the plane before and after the turbulence, as well as the reactions of the passengers. The story is structured according to the principles taught in class, with a beginning, middle, and end. It starts at the airport boarding gate and ends with an emergency landing at the nearest airport. The purpose of the story is to show the terror, dread, and relief that passengers feel during a flight emergency, as well as to convey the theme of survival and how people can overcome obstacles in such situations. The aim of the soundscape is to create an immersive and engaging experience for the listener, allowing them to feel like they are actually there and experiencing the emotions and sensations of the passengers on the plane.
